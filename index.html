QUICK START

Build a durable engine to reach 20.

  1. 01
    Start with cash and a Founder.

    The first company starts with $5; each later company starts with $1 more. Your Founder provides one permanent skill. Draft from the Talent Market or complete an Order or Milestone each turn.

  2. 02
    Choose how you scale.

    Agency and Staff cards provide 2–3 skill points but each costs $1 Upkeep. Shopify Tool cards provide one permanent skill with no Upkeep.

  3. 03
    Pay—or make cuts.

    At the start of your turn, pay the full Upkeep or discard Agency and Staff cards until you can afford it.

  4. 04
    Borrow carefully.

    A loan gives you $5 immediately but creates $8 of debt. Repaying $8 is free and does not use your turn. You cannot win while any loan remains.

  5. 05
    Your engine is persistent.

    Completing Orders and Milestones earns Cash, Reputation, or both without discarding engine cards. Each requires 3–8 total skill points, split across its listed skills. Some rewards are Cash-only or Reputation-only.

  6. 06
    Win with 20 reputation and no debt.

    Solo games finish immediately. Multiplayer games finish the current round, play one final round, and award the win to the most reputable debt-free company.

  7. 07
    Play against the Chaos Monkey solo.

    After every solo turn, the monkey takes one card from the Talent Market. It has a 1-in-5 chance to remove an open Order or Milestone instead. Optional event cards add even more chaos.
